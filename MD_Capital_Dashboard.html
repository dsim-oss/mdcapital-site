Investment Thesis
Core Thesis: Bitcoin is in a secular bull market driven by institutional adoption, sovereign accumulation, and monetary debasement. MD Capital captures this via a barbell strategy: direct BTC spot exposure (Swan) for pure upside + BTC-treasury equities (MSTR/STRC) for leveraged convexity and yield.

Edge: STRC provides ~11.5% yield anchored at $100 par, funded by Strategy's BTC-collateralized issuance engine. This generates income while maintaining BTC-correlated upside through the MSTR common equity position. The combination of spot BTC + high-yield preferred + common equity creates a full-stack BTC exposure with differentiated risk/return across the capital structure.
